Geoblog

Traditional travel blogs can be a great resource for travelers to find interesting things to do, but it's often difficult to get a sense of where different points of interests are, relative to one another. Is it feasible to go to this restaurant and visit this aquarium in the same day? Enter Geoblog: a travel blog website that uses Google Maps and Google Places API to address this problem by presenting a map view of different blog entries.
